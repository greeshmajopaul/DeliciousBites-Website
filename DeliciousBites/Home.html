About Us
History
M.R Abdullah founded MRA Bakery in 1965 on idyllic Indian coastal region of Malabar, once the epicenter of the old spice trade, the settlers from the Arab World, Portugal, Turkey and the Netherlands all helped create the delicious and unique dishes we see in the modern day state of Kerala, By Understanding homeland's tradition of fusion better than most, MRA's mission is to combine the best of Malabar cuisine with Western style bread and cakes to develop new and innovative recipes to the highest culinary standards. After 58 years of fine-tunning, product development, and loads of hard work, we have what you see today. Today, we serve excellent quality, fresh cooked meals and defined as a mainstream casual dining restaurant with an integrated bakery area tha caters to the community and local businesses. We currently based on 39 locations in both India and Qatar offering throughout the seven days a week, provide take out, fast pick-up and on site catering in addition to full table service. A Tradition Of Excellence!!!
Quality
Here at Paul’s Bakery we have always put quality ingredients first when producing cakes. We pride ourselves in the vast and loyal customer base we have built over the years. Having been open for such a long time, with experience on our side we have perfected fresh cream cakes with a taste second to none. We have developed an extensive selection of cake types with our 100% Fresh Cream cake range being the staple in our cakes. We have listened to customer requirements and offer sponge options and a selection of popular fillings to satisfy all tastebuds.
